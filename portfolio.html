The Challenge

New Steel Structures was managing $14M+ in projects across five states. The tools were solid—QuickBooks for accounting, Excel for analysis, SmartSheet for tracking. The problem? They didn't talk to each other.

  • Triple data entry: Every bill entered in QuickBooks, SmartSheet, and Excel
  • Stale data: Tuesday WIP reports were already a week old
  • 25-30 hours/week spent on manual reporting and data assembly

The Solution

A unified operations dashboard pulling directly from QuickBooks, serving as the single source of truth. Real-time visibility into WIP, cashflow, collections, and project health—automatically synced every night.

The 5-layer SSOT architecture ensures data flows one direction only. QuickBooks remains the source of truth. The frontend never calculates—it only displays.
